More inclusive camera

Google says it is creating a smartphone camera that more accurately depicts skin tone. "For people of colour, photography has not always seen us as we want to be seen, even in some of our own Google products" said Google's Sameer Samat. Google says it's making changes to auto white balance adjustments "to bring out natural brown tones". The company says it is also working on new algorithms to better distinguish the subject from a background. The camera will be ready on the new Google Pixel which will be out later this year.
